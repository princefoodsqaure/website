The Restaurant

Since 1994, we have been serving the people and have seen many ups and downs but your love for our restraunt has helped us to improve our service.

What today is known as 'Prince Food Square' just a small cart named 'Prince Ice Cream Parlour'.

The journey From 'Prince Ice Cream Parlour' to 'Prince Food Square' was not easy but we had a vison in mind which was to provide best possible service at the best possible rate.

We have been working to provide the best food possible in the town, and have improved a lot because of your constructive critisism.

The key ingredient for our success is your comments and coopertion of our staff. Whenever we got any suggestions from our customers we took it very seriously and worked on it to give the service which you expect.
